# Kimi Node Observation

## Purpose
本文件只记录 Kimi 作为稳定对接节点在真实运行中的关键样本，不做周报、不做路线图、不做泛化总结。

## Entry Rules

只允许记录以下三类：
- `boundary-overreach`
- `closure-success`
- `truth-state-correction`

每条记录必须包含：Date, Type, Context, What Kimi did, Why it matters, Boundary / Truth-state judgment, Follow-up.
