## 3. New Observation Structure

未来组织变革观察应采用双层结构。

### Macro Layer: Organizational Transformation

关注：

- large enterprise organizational restructuring;
- AI platform embedded into management and decision chains;
- accountability placement;
- platform governance;
- enterprise AI action surfaces;
- job compression and role recomposition;
- human-agent coordination at enterprise scale.

不是：

- generic "AI changes organizations" commentary;
- company PR;
- product review.

### Micro Layer: Work-Node / Small-Cut Observation

关注：

- how one concrete role changes;
- how one approval point changes;
- how maker-checker changes;
- how a dashboard or metric shapes human judgment;
- how an agent copilot changes responsibility path;
- how a workflow is rewritten;
- where closure moves.

不是：

- pure technical implementation;
- isolated product feature;
- psychological diagnosis.
